Looking for peace of mind when dating? Snug has your back! Snug - Safer Dating is an native app that helps people stay safe while dating. Download now and check it out.

Snug Features

What Makes Snug Cool

Date With A Safety Net

Create a date on our app and we'll help you stay safe. We'll check in with you after your date. If things go south don't worry, because your trusted contacts will be alerted. Date confidently with Snug.

Zero Ads

No one likes ads, especially us. Our user experience is our top priority and we want everyone to enjoy it without distrubance.

Completely Free

Safety should be free. Our main goal when creating Snug is to provide this tool to everyone. We want everyone to feel safe and enjoy life.

Your Privacy Matters

Snug collects and stores your profile information and data submitted about your dates. Thats it. We do not track you and believe that your data belongs to you, not us.

Simple & Easy

Using Snug could not get any easier. Our simple process of creating date only takes seconds. We designed it to be user friendly and accessible for all ages.

Cross-Platform

Why hinder a tool that can help people to one platform? That exactly what we thought and made Snug available on both Android and iOS. All future updates will be for both platforms.

Snug Roadmap

May 2021

Closed beta for Snug. Around 30 participants helped us catch bugs and fine-tune features in preparation for a public release. Infrastructure was tested behind the scenes.

June 2021

Public launch of Snug - Safer Dating. The app quickly spread to users across America through word of mouth and the use of social media.

September 2021

Sticking with our desire to give back, we sponsored multiple philanthropic events with university organizations to spread the word about safer dating.

January 2022

User feedback suggested a visual overhaul of Snug. We're coupling this with the most requested features. Snug 2.0 is expected to finish development in early 2022.

TBD

Users' most requested features implemented into Snug. These additional features improve date features and expand safety to additional lifestyles.
